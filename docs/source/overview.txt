SciKit-Fuzzy
============

Scikit-Fuzzy is a collection of fuzzy logic algorithms intended for use in the
`SciPy <http://scipy.org>`_ `Stack <http://www.scipy.org/stackspec.html>`_\ , written in the
`Python <http://www.python.org>`_ computing language.

This `SciKit <http://scikits.appspot.com>`_ is developed by the SciPy
community.  Contributions are welcome!  Please join us on the mailing list
or our persistent chatroom on Gitter.IM.
